Samosa Baozi

Notes

Oct 2 2022

I mostly followed this recipe for the samosa filling.

I also added potatoes and cauliflower. I cut the potatoes into small pieces and boiled them for about 8 minutes, and I roasted my cauliflower and cut it into small pieces. Then I added both to my samosa filling while it was sauteing. I also added a few raisins, but I don't really notice them.

I followed this recipe for the baozi dough.

Except I accidentally only added 1 cup of water instead of 1.5 cups while activating the yeast. But then while mixing the dough together it got really dry, so I added some more water again (maybe another full half cup, I'm not sure).

I also recommend adding a teaspoon of salt to the dough. I forgot to do that today, but I think it makes the dough taste better.
